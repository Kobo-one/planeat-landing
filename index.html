About

I dont want to eat this.

Young children tend to dislike trying out new food. It makes cooking for them stressful at times. You do not want a fight, but you also want your kids to try a variety of food and stay healthy. Especially because bad eating habits can lead to eating disorder in adulthood.

Fear no more. Planeat got you covered!

With the new app ‘Planeat’ we put an end to all these problems. We ensure that an evening meal is a moment of rest where everyone in the family is satisfied. A happy occation for both young and old.

Features

Trying new food

According to research kids have to try an ingredient up to 15 times before accepting it. Planeat helps you keep track of your kids’ growth to keep both you and your kids motivated.

Plan your meals

Sick of last-minute planning? Having trouble keeping track of what your kids already ate? Planeat gives you an overview of what your kids ate, how well they ate and what’s on the menu the next coming days.

Never forget what to buy

Humans makes mistakes sometimes. Planeat helps you prevent forgetting to buy that one important ingredient to make your favorite meals.

Gamify your meals

We know children are hard to convince. With our gamification system we try to give children input while maintaining your control on what will be put on the table. Give missions to your children and make their meals a fun and interactive experience.
